Basketball Bundesliga Lineups Version 1.0

Welcome, this app provides you with in-depth Basketball Bundesliga lineup statistics.

Based on a R-Shiny Framework, the data is updated weekly and the source code made available here.

Your Team

Investigate the lineup data of a specific team. Filter for a minimum number of possessions and, meanwhile, observe how many lineups you leave out .

Your Player(s)

Use a text-based search to find lineups with your players of choice.

Top Performances

Who are the five players forming the best offense, defense or both combined in the entire league? Use the interactive plot and classic top-10 lists to find out!

Statistics and Metrics used within this App Possessions: The number of both offensive and defensive possessions added up. Formally: FGA â€“ OREB + TO + 0,44*FTA Offensive Rating: The points a lineup scores per possession on offense. E.g. an ORtg of 1.12 equals 112 points averaged per 100 possessions Defensive Rating: The points a lineup allows per possession on defense. E.g. an DRtg of 1.12 equals 112 points allowed per 100 possessions on defense Net Rating: The difference between Offensive and Defensive Rating.
